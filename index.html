About me

Hello! My name is Ryan

Though I am a young designer, I have a variety of experience to pull from. In more short career, I have been afforded many opportunities to design and develop a variety of concepts for multiple projects. I love bringing ideas to life, and enjoy learning new skills to do so.

My Experience

  • I've done web design both front-end and web development.
  • I've coordinated photo and video product shoots, cut and edited videos, and key-framed animations.
  • I've designed t-shirts, posters, flyers, magazine add, brochures, and product manuals.

My skill sets

Software Skills

I fell in love with web design when I first learned Adobe Creative Suite particularly the Illustrator and Flash Components while in college. I then continued to broaden and improve my skills with Adobe Creative Suite while acquiring further experience with Sublime and Atom while working at Dougherty Forestry Manufacturing and through various freelance and contract web-design jobs.

Web Design Skills

I developed my earlier websites at the front end using css, html, flash, and javascript. My later websites required databases allowing me to learn PHP, MySQL, and AJAX.

Personal Skills

I enjoy web-designing and enjoy furthering my coding skills. I am too old (though not really) to sleep very long. I am proudly addicted to coffee. I also like to run and bike. I find these activities therapeutic.

Hello!

As a kid, I loved to draw. My favorite things where machines and systems. I liked to map out how things worked and display them in fun and quirky ways. I earned a graphic design degree in college, as the idea of marrying art and technology fascinated me. While working at a small manufacturing company called Dougherty Forestry, I found my niche. I was asked to create the company website. I found that my favorite aspect of web design was taking something from conception and creating a living and interactive space users could easily navigate with little effort. In this website, you will find some samplings of my work throughout the years. Feel free to browse at your leisure.
