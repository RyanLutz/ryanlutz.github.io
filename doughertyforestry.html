Dougherty Forestry Manufacturing

Dougherty Forestry Manufacturing is an Oklahoman company manufacturing and selling specialized forestry and agricultural equipment. I helped develop a company and brand image, designed digital and printed marketing material, and created a website to showcase their products.

A well known and sought after name in forestry equipment, Dougherty Forestry Manufacturing builds and distributes top of the line, land clearing and light forestry application attachments for skid steers, track loaders, excavators, and tractors. Dougherty Forestry Manufacturing required a variety of media needs from printed media such as: posters, flyers, banners, brochures, and product manuals, and digital media including: pictures touched up and exctracted, video, shot and edited, adna variety of web design needs.

  • Provided graphic design and web design services for two company websites in order to enhance community and online presence of company brand and services.
  • Developed company brand image and logo; designed printed media such as iers, banners, posters, brochures, and magazine ads.
  • Optimized websites using PageSeed and SEO tools; generated a 75% increase in website trafic through SEO and keyword optimization.
  • Organized personnel and purchased equipment for product photo shoots; obtained and edited product video.
  • Created Flash animations; created 3D renderings of products in Blender; extracted, masked, and enhanced product photography images.
  • Initiated and developed a content management system (CMS) to create and organize vendors, create news articles and manage trade show dates; developed (CMS) using PHP, MySQL, Maps API, and jQuery.
  • Researched VPS services and reported nding to supervisors to determine best VPS service; oversaw service transition.
  • Additional experience acquired through an outsourced project with Hinton Chamber of Commerce to create the Chamber’s website.

Dougherty Forestry Web Presence

turbosaw.com

ui/ux, html5/css3, responsive, CMS, PHP

Exciting customers about a large equipment purchase via the web, communicates the intent of Dougherty Forestry’s web presence and turbosaw.com. Customers can find detailed information about products offered by Dougherty Forestry, learn about forestry and invasive tree species, search for TurboSaw™ dealers near them, learn of upcoming shows and events Dougherty Forestry will participate in, and contact support for trouble shooting and service.

Dougherty Forestry Manufacturing's website, turbosaw.com, is designed to inform customers of products they manufacture, compare their products to the competition, and find a local TurboSaw™ dealer. The focus during development of the website was to lead customers with as few clicks as possible to the product that would best suite their needs.

Product Pages

Dealer Search

CMS, PHP/MySQL, Javascript/jQuery/ajax, google maps api

The dealer system allows users to quickly find dealers nearest to them either by address search or by Google Maps Location Services. The database is queried for the 10 closest dealers. The dealer database management system allows administrative users to quickly create a dealer by searching Google’s existing places database and auto fill address, website, and location information. Dealer participation can be managed quickly via a toggle button on the dealer list.

Custom CMS

Dougherty Forestry management personal can update the dealer database, create and update trade-show events, post news articles, and manage customer testimonials via a custom, light-weight csm tailored specifically for the company’s needs. Time and research proved that many pre-made cms solutions such as Wordpress and Magento were to bulky and complex for the desired outcomes, and a simple and precise php based, cms solution developed quickly with little to no external cost.
